The Day We Met

Nick and Sarah met on a online dating website. Nick messaged Sarah, she looked at his profile and messaged him back. After a few messages they exchanged numbers. Nick asked her on a date but she was going to be in Colorado for Christmas and scheduled a time to meet when she got back.

Their first date was on January 1, 2012 in Gilroy, CA. Sarah was living in Salinas and Nick was living in Sunnyvale so Gilroy was the halfway point between the two cities. They met at a cute little coffee shop and each had an iced tea. It was a warm New Years Day in California. After they went on a walk around downtown Gilroy. It was an educational walk about the history of Gilroy and laughed and talked.

After the walk they decided to get some food and found a sandwich shop. But at last it was time to go for Sarahâ€™s friend Michaela was in San Jose that evening and they were going to meet up. Sarah asked Nick if he would like to have a drink with Sarah and her friend later that evening. They had 2 dates on the same day. Wow! Nick gave Sarah a hug with a kiss on the cheek and that is how the story begins.

Nick is the CEO and Founder and Sarah is the Cook. Nick spends his days answering emails and in meetings. Sarah cooks lunch and dinner and spends the rest of her time blogging on her website.

Road Trip

This trip was a little more glamorous than the last one. We took the RV this time and glamped it up the California, Oregon, Washington coast, and back down. We spent a month on this trip, taking in all the sights and small towns all along the way. We visited Nick's Uncle Graham and his Aunt Miriam, who both graciously let us park our beast of an RV in front of their houses. We visited all over, from Olympic National Park to Pikes Place Market and everything in between. On our way home we stopped in Sarah's college town of Chico and visited the Hughes's Family. What a great trip! Can't wait for our next one!

2014
May

The Marriage Proposal

We had the top down in the cadillac and took all the back roads to Half Moon Bay. We cruised, talked and took our time. We parked when we got to the beach we had been at a few weeks prior. We got down to the sand and removed our shoes and started walking. Nick then said "We are not alone." I saw a canopy tent all set up with chairs, blankets and tiki torches. He then nudged me to go check it out with him, I was a little unsure. As we got closer, I got more nervous. I didn't want anyone to think we were trespassing on their stuff! Finally Nick asked me if the cooler looked familiar and realized it was for us! He told me Happy Birthday and we went and checked out the romantic canopy. There was wine, beer, cheese, crackers, fruit, chocolate, greek columns, candles, bean bag chairs and blankets. We grabbed a drink and snack and took a seat. We watched the waves and Charlie rolling in the sand. We walked up and down the beach and relaxed.

After a few hours, I was laying in the bean bag area and Nick came over to me. He came and laid down next to me and handed me a box. He told me to open it. After undoing the ribbon and opening the case, I think my jaw dropped to the sand. He asked if I would marry him! I of course said yes! He then proceeded to ask multiple times "For reals?" We spent the rest of the day enjoying the view and each other.
